Congratulations to Saloni Bhogale. Saloni was awarded the David Swanson Memorial Award today at [Throughput Computing Week 2026](https://agenda.hep.wisc.edu/event/2432/overview) for her resesarch utilizing throughput computing. Bhogale was an attendee of the OSG School and received the award as a former students who has achieved significant research results empowered by distributed high-throughput computing. Bhogale talked about her work 
"Justice at Scale: Leveraging High-Throughput Computing to Analyze Millions of Judicial Records."
